About

An independent audio studio from Seoul.

devbylaps was founded in 2026 as a focused, single-discipline studio: build clean, Apple-native audio tools — ship them under our own name — keep the catalog small enough that every plugin earns its place.

Mission

Most plugin companies grow by adding products. We grow by sharpening them. Each plugin we release is meant to do one specific job better than the generic options bundled with a DAW — whether that job is a quick mixing fix, a signature lo-fi character, or a dense tail for ambient work. We pick reverbs first because reverb is where most producers either over-spend (loading heavyweight CPU monsters) or under-spend (using the DAW stock for everything). We want a middle path.
