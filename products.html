Catalog

Four reverbs. Four jobs.

Every devbylabs plugin is shipped as a real AUv3 binary — loadable in Logic Pro, GarageBand, AUM, and any AUv3-compatible DAW on macOS and iOS. All releases are signed with Apple Developer ID and notarized.

LushVerb

Beta

Dense, modulated tail reverb.

LushVerb is built for the moments where the reverb is the part: ambient pads, vocal tails, suspended chord layers. A long modulated tail sits behind shorter pre-delay and a gentle high-shelf damping so the reverb sits behind the source without smearing transients.

Format
AUv3
Platforms
macOS 13+, iOS 17+
Status
Beta — open testing

GyuZZangVerb

In development

A signature character reverb — not a utility.

GyuZZangVerb leans into character. Intentionally smeared, slightly broken, lo-fi at the edges. It is the kind of plugin you grab when the track needs a sound, not a clean reverb. Best on drums, guitars, and anything that benefits from a touch of grime.

Format
AUv3
Platforms
macOS 13+, iOS 17+
Status
In development — internal alpha

TheFixVerb

In development

Quick-fix mixing reverb — two knobs, sensible defaults.

TheFixVerb is the boring one, on purpose. When you do not want to think, when the vocal is dry and the project is due, drop in TheFixVerb, set size and mix, move on. A JUCE build is also being produced so it can ship as a VST3 alongside the AUv3 version.

Format
AUv3 (Apple-native build), VST3 (JUCE build)
Platforms
macOS 13+, iOS 17+, Windows 10+ (JUCE only)
Status
In development

SimpleReverb

Released

One algorithm, two knobs, no menus.

SimpleReverb is the entry point to the devbylabs catalog. It is what a reverb looks like with everything inessential removed: one decay knob, one mix knob, one stereo image. Useful as a sketch reverb on iPad with AUM, or as a fast utility insert when nothing else needs to happen.

Format
AUv3
Platforms
macOS 13+, iOS 17+
Status
Released
