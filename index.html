What we do

Native audio plugins

We design AUv3 plugins that run inside Logic Pro, GarageBand, AUM, and any AUv3-aware DAW on macOS and iOS. All our plugins are written against Apple's first-party audio frameworks — no third-party plugin SDK, no licensing layers, no hidden telemetry.

iOS & macOS apps

Beyond plugins, we ship native iOS and macOS apps — companion utilities for musicians and producers, plus focused tools for everyday creative work. Built in Swift and SwiftUI, Apple Silicon native, distributed through the App Store and our own signed, notarized channels on macOS.

Plugin catalog

How we work

  • Apple-native. Built on AudioUnit, Core Audio, SwiftUI — no Electron, no third-party DSP middleware.
  • Signed & notarized. Every macOS release is Developer ID signed and notarized by Apple.
  • No telemetry. Our plugins do not phone home. Ever.
  • Made in Seoul. Designed and engineered in South Korea, tuned for international workflows.
